Cell Evolution is the first puzzle sandbox dapp base on Nebulas, each player act as a cell group. Players need to balance the Adaptability, Reproductivity and Survivability in the game. When player finished the game, they can choose to do the DNA merging, this function will upload the data into the whole ethnic group. This is not only a game, but also a read social experiment. If the whole ethnic group have unbalance data, the whole world will destroy. Millions of the cells will decide the whole destiny of the world. Welcome to Cell Evo, a game about cells and humanity;
